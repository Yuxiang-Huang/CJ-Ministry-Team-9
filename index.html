CJ Ministry

"Where Hands Gather in Prayer"

Our Mission Statement

CJ Ministry's mission seeks to proclaim Spiritual Brokenness and social Pain with Faith, Love, and Hope.

About Us

This organization goes beyond the building, providing services within the community while focusing on renewing minds, transforming lives with spiritual solutions to worldly problems, and providing resources, whether bridging the gap for people experiencing homelessness or giving weekly check-ins for the elderly, working through barriers for those who have a mental illness or substance abuse to help them work toward rebuilding themselves, their family's. We travel and want to meet you where you are, which allows us to lead you toward a relationship with Christ while providing these resources within our communities where people are most vulnerable due to lifestyle or situations.

Leadership

Pastor Candace Jones is the founder and an Ordained Minister through Open Ministry. Pastor Jones is not a state-licensed therapist; she is a certified Christian Counselor through the National Association of Christian Counselors and a Certified Pre-Marriage Counselor through Prepare-Enrich Facilitator. Pastor Jones is also a Mental health Coach through The American Association of Christian Counselors and a Certified Adult Mental Health First Aid and has been trained to provide initial help to someone experiencing a mental health or substance use challenge.

Our Values

Honor God

Honor God through prayer, and reading God's Word.

Spread Love

Love each other and the community. We believe that we are all broken and need healing and growth.

Diversity

Value cultural diversity and building a diverse space for clients and the community we serve.
